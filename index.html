Who We Are

Hilmaamis Construction & General Service Company is a trusted leader in the construction industry, proudly serving communities since 2012.
Headquartered in Baidoa City, Somalia, with a strategic presence in Nairobi, Kenya, we specialize in delivering innovative, high-quality, and transformative construction solutions that exceed expectations.
Our expertise spans a diverse range of services, including the construction of residential homes, commercial buildings, modern offices, restaurants, and industrial facilities.
Additionally, we excel in infrastructure development, renovation and remodeling, and custom-designed projects tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.
At Hilmaamis, we are driven by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction.
We combine regional expertise with global standards, leveraging cutting-edge technology, sustainable practices, and a highly skilled workforce to bring visions to life.
Whether building homes, commercial spaces, or public infrastructure, we strive to shape the future of construction with quality, integrity, and efficiency as our foundation.
